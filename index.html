How to Care for Your Garments

Washing

Machine wash cold with similar colors. Use mild detergent and avoid bleach.

Drying

Tumble dry on low heat or hang dry. Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Ironing

Iron on medium heat if needed. Turn garments inside out to protect prints.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Fold heavy items and hang delicate pieces.
